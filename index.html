Grégoire Aufort

Researcher in Statistics and astronomy

Quick presentation

I am a postdoctoral resarcher at the Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris.
My research focuses on developing advanced computational methods to analyze large astronomical datasets, particularly through Bayesian inference and machine learning techniques, with a focus on studying galaxies properties and their environment.
Previously, I completed my Statistics Ph.D. at Aix-Marseille University, where I was part of the Institut de Mathématiques de Marseille and the Laboratoire d'Astrophysique de Marseille, with a thesis titled titled 'Bayesian Computational Statistics to study Spectral Energy Distributions of galaxies'.
I'm currently involved in the COSMOS collaboration and the Euclid Consortium.
